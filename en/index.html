About Me


Abrahan Blasco is a venezuelan luthier, musician guitarist and trumpet player. He studied chemistry and mathematics at UCLA and at IUTY, besides being a turner and artistic artist.

The materials used in his instruments, among others, include miguelito, curari, algarobo, old mahogany, pines and spruces of german origin. All the used frets are made of Steward Macdonald stainless steel, thus achieving perfect tuning in each fret and chord.

Abrahan also manufactures the midi cuatro, an innovative system that uses the rmc system of godin guitars. On the other hand the rings of its cuatros have greater thickness (5mm) producing an instrument with sound more round and powerful.
